Settings & Preferences

The Settings module is the company's "Command Center." It’s the one place where every employee can make the app their own. Update your profile, choose how you want to be notified, and even switch the whole app to a stylish Dark Mode with a single click.

04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

Logic for the Command Center:

Staggered Cards

We use a StaggerContainer to animate the entry. When you open settings, the cards (Profile, Security, Appearance) pop in one-by-one sequentially.

Theme Toggling

We use one line of code: classList.toggle("dark"). It adds or removes the "dark" theme class from the top of the HTML page in real-time.

Local State

Changes are held in a profile object. They only become permanent once you click the "Save Profile" button, triggering a success toast.

Toast Feedback

We used the useToast hook to give you instant confirmation message (like "Profile Updated") at the bottom of the screen.

// Theme Toggling Logic
onCheckedChange={(v) => document.documentElement.classList.toggle("dark", v)}
05. How to use this page

Setting up your workspace? Here’s the best way to leverage Settings:

STEP 1: UPDATE YOUR ID

Make sure your **Job Title** and **Phone** are correct. This ensures the Org Chart and People directory are always up to date.

STEP 2: FIX YOUR THEME

Scroll down to the **Appearance** section. If the white screen is too bright, toggle the **Dark Mode** switch to save your eyes.

STEP 3: SAVE YOUR CHANGES

Don't forget to click the **Save** button in each section. Look for the floating "Success" message to be sure it worked!
