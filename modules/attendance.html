Module 06: The Timecard

Attendance

The Attendance module is the company’s digital timecard. It’s where employees check in when they start work and check out when they finish. It helps everyone stay on schedule and makes sure that work hours are tracked accurately.

01. The Clock-In Station

When you open the Attendance page, the first thing you see is a big, real-time clock. It tells you exactly what time it is, down to the second!

03. History & Daily Logs

The Calendar

You can click any day on the calendar to see who worked on that specific date. It’s a fast way to check attendance for last Tuesday or any other day in the past.

A simple table that lists every employee's name, their check-in time, their check-out time, and their final status (like "Present" or "Late").

06. How to use this page

Starting or ending your day? Here’s how to use the Attendance tracker:

STEP 1: CHECK IN

When you arrive, click the Check In button. The clock will record your exact start time in green.

STEP 2: SCAN THE LOG

If you are a manager, check the scoreboard to see who has arrived and who might be running late.

STEP 3: CHECK OUT

Before you leave, don't forget to click Check Out. This finishes your daily log for the system.

Privacy Note

Safety first! Regular employees can only see their own attendance logs. Managers can see the daily logs for their entire team to help with payroll and scheduling.
