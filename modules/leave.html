Module 21: The Out-of-Office Portal

Leave Management

The Leave Management module is the company's "Holiday Planner." It’s where employees can book time off for vacations, sick days, or personal matters, and where managers can approve requests to keep the team running smoothly. It’s designed to balance work and well-being.

01. Requesting Time Off

When you need a break, use the **New Leave Request** form. It’s simple: pick a category (like Annual Leave), choose your dates, and add a brief reason. Your manager will be notified as soon as you hit the "Submit" button.

03. Manager Approvals

Role-Based Controls

Managers and the HR team have extra buttons! On any **Pending** request, they can click "Approve" or "Reject." Regular employees **never** see these buttons, keeping the system fair and professional.

04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

How we handle the holiday workflow:

Privacy Logic

We use visibleRequests to ensure employees only see their own rows, while managers have the "Master View."

Status Styles

A simple statusStyle object maps the three statuses (Approved, Pending, Rejected) to their CSS colors automatically.

Interactive Buttons

The "Approve/Reject" buttons only exist for users with Admin or Manager roles, keeping the UI clean for staff.

Table Wrapper

We use a TableWrapper component to ensure the list of requests stays scrollable and tidy on mobile devices.

05. How to use this page

Planning a vacation? Here’s the best way to leverage the Leave module:

STEP 1: SUBMIT THE FORM

Pick your start and end dates carefully. Always double-check if your dates overlap with a major company event!

STEP 2: WAIT FOR PENDING

Keep an eye on the **Leave Requests** table. Your new entry will stay yellow (Pending) until a manager reviews it.

STEP 3: GET APPROVAL

Once the badge turns green (Approved), your leave is officially booked in the **Company Calendar** automatically.
