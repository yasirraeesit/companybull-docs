Module 14: The Personal Portfolio

Employee Profile

The Employee Profile is a "Deep Dive" into every person at Company Bull. It’s where we keep a detailed history of their career, their technical skills, and their important documents. It’s designed to help team members track their growth and managers prepare for performance reviews.

01. The Profile Sidebar

On the left side of the page, we show the "Essentials." This includes the employee’s photo (avatar), job title, department, and contact details. It’s the first thing you see and gives you the most important facts immediately.

04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

How we built this high-fidelity profile view:

Axios Data Fetch

We use axios.get() and the id from the URL to load every unique employee's data from our backend API.

Tabs Logic

The TabsContent components handle the heavy lifting of hiding and showing the Timeline vs the Skills without reloading the page.

Timeline Icons

A simple "Icon Mapping" object (timelineIcons) tells the system which emoji (🎉 or 🚀) to use based on the event type.

Safety Guards

The code includes a !hasAccess check to ensure that employees cannot "spy" on other people's profiles unless they are Managers or HR.

// Grabbing the ID from the URL

05. How to use this page

Want to know your team better? Here’s the best way to leverage the Employee Profile:

STEP 1: REVIEW THE HISTORY

Before a 1-on-1 meeting, check the **Timeline** to see the employee’s journey. It’s a great way to start a conversation about their growth.

STEP 2: FIND SKILL GAPS

Check the **Skills** tab. If a team member has high scores in a specific area, they can act as a "Mentor" for others.

STEP 3: DOWNLOAD FILES

(HR only) Use the **Documents** tab to find old contracts or medical notes without leaving the employee’s profile.
