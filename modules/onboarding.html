04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

Logic for the checklist engine:

Set-Based State

We use a new Set() to track IDs. It’s faster and more efficient for adding or removing "Completed" tasks than a standard list.

Progress Math

The system calculates (completed.size / totalSteps) * 100 to find the overall score. We also use .filter() to find "Required" items.

Category Map

A simple categoryColors object maps each task type (IT, Profile, etc.) to its specific CSS color automatically.

Conditional Opacity

When you finish a task, we change the card's opacity to 70% and add a line-through class to focus your eyes on what's left.

// Toggle logic with Set
const next = new Set(prev);
next.has(id) ? next.delete(id) : next.add(id);
05. How to use this page

Starting your new job? Here’s the best way to leverage Onboarding:

STEP 1: DO REQUIRED FIRST

Always start with the tasks marked in red (**Required**). These are essential for things like getting paid or getting your laptop.

STEP 2: CLICK TO COMPLETE

Once you finish a step (like signing a paper), click the checkbox. The green checkmark will appear, and your scores will update.

STEP 3: ENJOY THE VIEW

Your goal is to reach 100% on the **Overall Progress** bar. Once you do, you’re officially fully onboarded!
