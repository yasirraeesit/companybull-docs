Module 24: The Company Tree

Organization Chart

The Org Chart is the company's "Family Tree." It’s an interactive map that shows how everyone in the company is connected. It helps you see who reports to whom and understand the bigger picture of our organization from the CEO all the way down to every team member.

01. The Team Member Card

Every person in the company is represented by a "Node." These cards show a small **Avatar** (for their face), their **Name**, their **Job Title**, and a **Department Badge**. We’ve kept the design clean so you can see many people at once without clutter.

02. Unlocking the Tiers

The chart is **Expandable**. If a manager has a team, you’ll see a small chevron icon (down or right). Click on their card, and the next level of the tree will "Slide Out." This allows the chart to stay organized while still holding hundreds of people.

03. The Visual Web (Connectors)

Drawing the Connections

We use 1px vertical and horizontal lines to draw the "Reporting Paths." Even as the team grows wider, these lines automatically stretch and center themselves, ensuring that the hierarchy is always crystal clear.

04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

How we built the recursive tree:

Recursive Node

We built one component (OrgNode) that calls itself if it finds a "Children" array. This allows the tree to grow infinitely without writing new code for each level.

Default Expand

To make the chart useful immediately, we automatically expand levels 0 and 1, so you see the leadership team as soon as you open the page.

Line Math (Calc)

We use calc() logic in CSS to find the exact center of child nodes, ensuring the connecting lines are perfectly pixel-aligned.

Avatar Fallback

If a user doesn't have a photo, we use a simple **AvatarFallback** that generates a colorful circle with their initials inside.

05. How to use this page

New to the team? Here’s the best way to explore the Org Chart:

STEP 1: START AT THE TOP

Begin at the root (the CEO) and follow the lines down to understand the different departments and leadership tiers.

STEP 2: CLICK TO DRILL DOWN

If you want to see who reports to a specific Director, just click their card. The branch will expand to show their team.

STEP 3: SCROLL SIDEWAYS

Big teams (like Engineering) can be very wide! Use your mouse or trackpad to scroll left and right to see everyone on that tier.
