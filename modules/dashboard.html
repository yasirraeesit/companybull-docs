04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

How we built this high-performance landing page:

Recharts Lab

We use Recharts for every visual. They are "Responsive," meaning they resize perfectly for mobile phones or huge monitors.

Role Filtering

The visibleStats array uses a role-check to hide sensitive data (like Salary) if you are just a regular Employee.

Framer Motion

The small "slide-in" effects on the stats and activity use StaggerContainer to make the page feel alive.

Custom Tooltips

Every chart has a custom Tooltip that uses the Neo-Brutalist shadow and font style to match our app.

05. How to use this page

Ready to direct your team? Here’s the best way to leverage the Dashboard:

STEP 1: SCAN THE KPIS

Check the top row of cards first. If any color turns red or shows a downward trend, you should investigate those modules immediately.

STEP 2: PIPELINE CHECK

Admins and HR should check the Recruitment Pipeline pie chart to see how many people are stuck in the "Interview" phase.

STEP 3: ACTIVITY CATCH-UP

Before starting your tasks, scroll the Recent Activity list at the bottom to see what has changed since you last logged in.
