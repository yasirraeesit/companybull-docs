Module 03: The Reports Section

Analytics & Reports

The Analytics module is like a dashboard for your company. It shows you everything from how many people work in each department to how much you’re spending on salaries. We use clear charts to make it easy to see trends and make decisions.

02. Workforce Insights

Headcount by Department

This bar chart shows how many employees are in each department (like Engineering, HR, or Sales). It’s perfect for seeing which teams are growing the fastest.

A pie chart that breaks down who is Active, On Leave, or Inactive. This helps HR understand current workforce availability at a glance.

A multi-layered Area Chart that tracks two things over 6 months:

  • Total Spend The primary base salary for all employees.

04. Attendance & Leave Pulse

Today's Attendance

A real-time donut chart showing who is Present, Late, Absent, or On Leave today.

Leave Request Status

A bar chart tracking pending vs approved vs rejected leave requests. This helps the HR team stay on top of their to-do list.

05. Interactive Tools

Period Selection

Users can change the entire dashboard's context using the Period Selector. You can view data for 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, or 1 year. The graphs update automatically!

Reports Export

Each section has a Download button. While currently in demo mode (showing a toast notification), this is designed to generate CSV or PDF files of the specific data you're looking at.

08. How to use this page

Ready to pull some reports? Here is the best way to use the Analytics dashboard:

STEP 1: SET THE DATE

Use the dropdown at the top right to choose a time period (like "Last 6 Months"). This scales all charts instantly.

STEP 2: SWITCH TABS

Click between Workforce, Payroll, or Attendance to see different types of charts and data lists.

STEP 3: EXPORT DATA

Found a chart you like? Click the Download button to save the data to your computer for an spreadsheet or meeting.

Fun Fact

Did you know? All the charts on this page use the same HSL color system as the rest of the app. This ensures that a "Sales" bar here looks exactly like a "Sales" badge on the employee list!
