Module 26: The Hiring Hub

Recruitment

The Recruitment module is the company's "Grow Gear." It’s where HR and Admins track every open job role and watch as candidates move from "Applied" to "Hired." It’s designed to ensure we find the best talent and keep the hiring process moving fast.

01. Active Job Board

When we need a new team member, it starts here. The **Open Positions** table lists every active job role. You can see the department, where the role is located (like Remote or NY), and exactly how many people have already sent in their resumes.

03. High-Security Hiring

Access Restricted

Hiring is sensitive! Regular employees and even some managers cannot enter this portal. Only the **HR** and **Admin** teams have the "Keys" to see candidate names and job statuses. If anyone else tries to enter, the system blocks them automatically.

04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

How we handle the recruitment flow:

Staggered Pipelines

We use StaggerContainer to animate the stages. When you load the page, the cards slide in one by one from left to right (Applied → Hired).

Stage Styling

The STAGE_COLORS array maps each stage to its own signature color, like Orange for Interviews and Green for Offers.

Auth Gating

We check hasAccess("Admin", "HR") at the top of the file. If you don't have permission, the app returns a "Blocked" screen before any data is even fetched.

Candidate Mapping

The pipeline is "Nested." We loop through the **Stages**, and then inside each stage, we loop through the **Candidates** to draw their name tags.

05. How to use this page

Running the hiring team? Here’s the best way to leverage Recruitment:

STEP 1: ADD A JOB ROLE

Click the **+ New Posting** button at the top right to start a search for a new team member. It will appear in the table instantly.

STEP 2: WATCH THE PIPELINE

Check the **Hiring Pipeline** every morning. If a candidate is stuck in "Phone Screen" for a week, reach out to their recruiter!

STEP 3: SEND AN OFFER

Once a candidate finishes their final interview, move them to the green **Offer** stage to start the background check.
