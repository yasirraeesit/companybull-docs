Welcome to Company Bull

Making Teamwork Simple & Fast

Hi there! Company Bull is a clean, modern tool designed to help you manage your team and your company's operations without the headache. We built it for developers and managers who want a single place for everything—from hiring and payroll to tracking daily tasks.

What is Company Bull?

Built for Humans

Most company management tools are cluttered and confusing. We made Company Bull easy to use. No hidden menus, no tiny text—just clear, bold information that helps you get things done.

Fast & Reliable

We used the best technology (React and Node.js) to make sure the app never slows you down. Whether you’re checking a report or adding a new employee, it happens instantly.

Why does it look like this?

You’ll notice thick borders and bold shadows. This is a design style called "Neo-Brutalism." We use it because it makes the app extremely clear. Every button and every card is easy to see, so you never get lost.

Quick Start for Developers

Ready to jump in? Here is how you can get started in just a few minutes:

1. The Setup

Download the code, install the dependencies with npm install, and make sure you have MongoDB running.

2. Explore

Look through the 29 modules in the sidebar. Each one is a different part of the app, like "Analytics" or "Payroll."

3. Build

Choose a module and start coding! We use a simple pattern for every page, so it’s easy to add new features.

Need Help?
