Module 11: The Team Map

Departments

The Departments module provides a high-level view of how the company is organized. It’s a clean grid that lists every team, who leads them, and how many people work there. It’s perfect for understanding the structure of the business at a single glance.

01. Anatomy of a Team Card

Every team has its own "Card." We designed these cards to be simple so you can find the most important information—like the manager or the headcount—in less than a second.

03. Budget Transparency

Visibility is key for leaders. If you are an **Admin** or **HR**, you will see the annual budget for each department in the top right corner of the card. Managers can see the cards but they cannot see the budget numbers.

04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

Here’s the developer’s "recipe" for the organizational grid:

Color Trick

We use dept.color + "1a" to create a 10% transparent background of the department's main color for a soft "Glow" look.

Initial Generator

We use split(" ") and map(n[0]) to turn a manager's full name (like "John Doe") into initials ("JD") for the avatar.

Safety Lock

Before any code runs, a top-level if (!hasAccess) check verifies the role. If it fails, the rest of the page is never even loaded.

Staggered Load

We wrap our grid in StaggerContainer so the departments slide into view one-by-one with a smooth animation.

05. How to use this page

Need to understand our team structure? Here’s the best way to use the Departments view:

STEP 1: CHECK HEADCOUNT

Scan the cards to see which teams are the largest and which are the smallest. This helps with resource planning.

STEP 2: FIND A MANAGER

If you need to reach out to a team lead, use the avatar section at the bottom of the card to find out who is in charge.

STEP 3: VIEW BUDGETS

(Admins only) Use the top right numbers to ensure your department spending is aligned with the annual plan.
