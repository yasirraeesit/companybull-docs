04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

How we handle the financial logic:

Summing Engine

We use .reduce() to add up every dollar amount in the array to give you those "Total" and "Pending" scoreboard numbers.

Conditional Headers

The "Employee" column in the table is conditional. It only shows up for Admins/Managers so employees don't see who else is getting reimbursed.

Dual Search

The search bar is smart: it checks both the **Category** (like Travel) and the **Description** (like Flight to London) at the same time.

Amount Formatting

We use .toLocaleString() to make sure those big numbers have commas (like $10,000) for easy reading.

// Calculating the Pending balance (Logic)

05. How to use this page

Ready to get your money back? Here’s the best way to leverage the Expenses hub:

STEP 1: SUBMIT EARLY

Click the blue **+ Submit Expense** button as soon as you have your receipt. Don't wait until the end of the month!

STEP 2: CHECK THE STATUS

Use the **Status Dropdown** to see if your claim is still "Pending." If it’s "Rejected," reach out to HR for more details.

STEP 3: (FOR MANAGERS)

Check the Scoreboard daily. If the "Pending" total is high, it means your team is waiting on you for their reimbursements.
