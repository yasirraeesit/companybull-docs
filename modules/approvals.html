Module 05: The Approvals Manager

Approvals

The Approvals module is the company’s task-checker. It’s where managers and administrators review requests for things like time off, work expenses, or new equipment. It makes sure every decision is tracked and nothing gets forgotten.

01. At-a-Glance Dashboard

At the top of the page, there are three colorful cards that show the current status of all requests. This helps you quickly see how much work is left to do.

Pending

Waiting for a decision

Approved

Work is finished

Rejected

Request denied

02. Pending vs. Processed

Pending Tab

This is your "To-Do" list. It shows every request that needs an answer right now. If there are no requests, you'll see a friendly "All caught up!" message.

Processed Tab

This is your "Archive." Once a request is approved or rejected, it moves here. It’s a great way to look back at past decisions for auditing.

07. How to use this page

Need to clear your desk? Here’s how to handle approval requests:

STEP 1: VIEW PENDING

Start on the Pending tab. This is where all new requests show up for your review.

STEP 2: TAKE ACTION

Read the request details and click Approve or Reject. The item will vanish and move to the archive.

STEP 3: CHECK HISTORY

Switch to the Processed tab if you ever need to see a record of what you decided in the past.

Quick Tip

We use the EmptyState component to make the page look clean when you are all caught up. No messy blank pages, just a clear "All caught up!" message.
