04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

Logic for the mission control board:

Drag-and-Drop

We use onDragStart to "remember" which task is being moved, and onDrop to update its status in the database instantly.

Kanban Map

The board is generated from a statusColumns array. This makes it easy to add new columns (like "Testing") in just one line of code.

Task Filtering

We use items.filter() to check your ID. If you aren't an Admin, the system only shows you tasks where YOUR name is on the card.

Priority Colors

A priorityStyle object maps "High" to red and "Low" to green, so you can tell at a glance which tasks are most urgent.

05. How to use this page

Starting your day? Here’s the best way to leverage Tasks & Projects:

STEP 1: FIND YOUR TASKS

Check the **To Do** column. Look for your name on the cards to see what projects you're assigned to today.

STEP 2: DRAG TO START

As soon as you open a file to start working, grab the card and drag it into the **In Progress** column. This lets the team know you're on it!

STEP 3: FINISH & CELEBRATE

Once the job is done, drop it in the **Done** column. The card will turn green and be added to the company's finished missions list.
