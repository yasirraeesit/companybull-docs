Module 04: The Newsroom

Announcements

The Announcements module is where the whole company stays connected. It’s like a digital bulletin board where you can see the latest news, updates from HR, or technical wins from the engineering team. We made it clear and easy to read so you never miss an important update.

01. Important News Stays at the Top

Some news is too important to miss! We use a "Pinning" system to make sure critical updates stay at the very top of the list, even when new messages are added.

Pinned Posts

If an announcement is "Pinned," it gets a special light-purple background and a pin icon. This tells everyone, "Hey, read this first!"

Regular News

Normal updates appear below the pinned ones in chronological order. They are clean, simple, and easy to scroll through.

02. Colored Categories

To help you find what you’re looking for, every announcement has a colored "Badge." This tells you which department the news is coming from.

03. Posting New Updates

The "New Announcement" Button

If you are an Admin, HR, or Manager, you will see a "New Announcement" button. When you click it, a simple form slides down so you can write your message.

  • Title Grab people's attention.
  • Content Write the full message.
  • Publish Send it to the board instantly!

06. How to use this page

Want to stay in the loop? Here’s how to use the Announcements board:

STEP 1: CHECK THE PINNED

Always look at the top of the list first. If there’s urgent news, it will be "Pinned" there with a special icon.

STEP 2: SCAN THE COLORS

Look at the colored badges to quickly find news from your specific department or from HR.

STEP 3: POST A MESSAGE

If you’re a manager, use the Plus (+) button to start writing. Your team will see it instantly!

Quick Tip

Announcements use the Avatar component. If an author doesn't have a photo, it automatically shows their initials (like "JD" for John Doe) in the primary color!
