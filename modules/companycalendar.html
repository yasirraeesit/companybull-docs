05. How to use this page

Need to plan your week? Here’s the best way to use the Company Calendar:

STEP 1: SYNC YOUR WEEK

Check the grid every Monday morning to see if there are any department-wide meetings you need to prepare for.

STEP 2: WATCH FOR DEADLINES

Look for the red **⚠️ Deadline** badges. These are non-negotiable project dates that the whole team is tracking.

STEP 3: PLAN FOR HOLIDAYS

Switch to future months to see when the office will be closed for public holidays so you can plan your own personal leave around them.
