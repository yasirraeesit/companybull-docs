Module 22: The Front Door

Welcome & Login

The Login module is the company's "First Impression." Instead of a boring password form, we’ve built a high-energy "Role Picker" for our demo. This lets you jump straight into the action by picking the type of user you want to be—whether it’s a powerful Admin or a busy Employee.

01. Choose Your Persona

When you first arrive, you’ll see four colorful role buttons. Each one is a "Key" to a different part of the app. Every card has a short description saying exactly what that person can do, making it easy to decide who you want to be today.

03. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

Logic for the entry system:

Demo Auto-Login

We use a custom loginAs(role) function. When you click a button, it immediately sets your permissions and redirects you to the home page.

Motion Elements

The role buttons use framer-motion with a staggered delay (i * 0.1), so they pop into view one-by-one when the page loads.

Interactive Depth

The buttons use a translate effect. When you hover, the card "sinks" into its shadow, creating a satisfying 3D "Click" feeling.

Logo Inversion

We use filter: invert() on the bull logo to make sure it looks great against both light and dark backgrounds without needing two files.

04. How to use this page

Ready to explore the app? Here’s the best way to get started:

STEP 1: BROWSE THE ROLES

Take a second to read the descriptions. If you want to see everything, pick **Admin**. If you want a simpler view, pick **Employee**.

STEP 2: CLICK TO ENTER

Click the card that matches your needs. The "ArrowRight" icon will glow, and you’ll be teleported straight to the Dashboard.

STEP 3: EXPLORE THE VIEW

Once inside, look around! Most pages will show or hide buttons based on the role you picked at the login screen.
