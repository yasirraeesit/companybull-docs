Module 15: The Workforce Directory

Employees

The Employees module is the "Main Directory" of Company Bull. This is the place where every single team member is listed, managed, and hired. It’s designed to give managers total control over their workforce and help employees find their co-workers quickly.

01. Finding the Right Person

If you have a big team, you don’t want to scroll forever. We’ve built a "Smart Filter" system at the top of the page. You can search by **Name** or **Job Title**, and narrow things down by **Department** or **Role** instantly.

03. The Hiring & Editing Flow

Admins and HR have special "Super Powers" on this page. They can add new employees or update existing ones using the **Add Employee** and **Edit** buttons. These buttons open a "Dialog" (a popup box) that lets you change roles, status, or job titles.

New Hire

The "Add" form makes sure you collect all the essential data: Name, Email, Job Title, and Role.

Updating Details

If someone changes their job title or moves to a new department, just click the "Pencil" icon to update them instantly.

Full Profile

The "Eye" icon takes you to the deep-dive profile where you can see their skills and documents.

04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

How we built this high-performance directory:

API CRUD

We use axios.get(), axios.post(), and axios.put() to connect directly to the backend database at :5000/api/employees.

Framer Motion

Every row in the table uses a motion.tr stagger effect, making the list slide in one-by-one when you open the page.

Access Control

The baseList logic ensures that regular employees only see their own entry, while managers sees the whole team.

Dialog States

We use local states (isAddDialogOpen, isEditDialogOpen) to manage the visibility of the popup forms.

// Adding a new employee (API logic)

05. How to use this page

Keeping your team organized? Here’s the best way to leverage the Employees hub:

STEP 1: ADD YOUR NEW HIRES

Click the blue **+ Add Employee** button as soon as a new contract is signed. This gives them instant access to the portal.

STEP 2: MANAGE STATUS

If someone goes on parental leave, use the "Edit" tool to change their status to **On Leave** to keep the department data accurate.

STEP 3: QUICK VIEW

Use the **Eye** icon instead of the "Edit" pencil if you just want to read someone's phone number or check their job title.
