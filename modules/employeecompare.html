Employee Comparison

The Employee Comparison tool is the "Battle of the Best." It’s a side-by-side view where HR and Managers can pick any two team members and see exactly how they stack up in terms of performance, salary, and technical expertise. It’s perfect for making fair promotion decisions!

01. The Selection Bar

At the top of the page, you’ll find two simple dropdown menus. Use these to pick the two employees you want to compare. As soon as you select a second name, the entire page updates automatically with their "Battle Stats."

05. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

How we built the comparison engine:

Highlighting Engine

We use a custom compareRow helper. It compares the two values and adds a text-primary font-bold class based on which value is higher.

Skill Union

Since different people have different skills, we use new Set() to merge their skill lists into one unique list for the UI.

Progress Bars

The Skill-Gap view uses dual Progress components from our UI library, letting you see technical expertise as a percentage.

Data Linking

This module is heavily linked. It pulls basic data from employees.json, skill data from skills.json, and pay data from payroll.json.

06. How to use this page

Conducting a performance review? Here’s the best way to leverage the comparison tool:

STEP 1: CHOOSE CONTENDERS

Pick two employees from the same level (e.g., two Senior Devs) to see who is performing more effectively.

STEP 2: CHECK SALARY EQUITY

Compare their "Net Pay" to ensure that people in similar roles and with similar skills are being paid fairly.

STEP 3: IDENTIFY GAPS

Look for "Empty Skills" (the gray lines). If one person is missing a skill that the other has, you can plan training for them.
