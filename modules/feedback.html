04. Engineering Blueprint

Behind the Scenes (Code)

Logic for the feedback engine:

Interactive Stars

We use onMouseEnter to update a "Hover Score," making stars light up in yellow *before* you even click them.

Status Logic

We use a statusConfig object to map "Survey Status" (Active, Completed) to their specific icons and colors automatically.

Response %

The progress bars are driven by simple math: Math.round((responses / total) * 100).

Toast Feedback

Once you hit "Submit," a toast triggers to confirm anonymity, and the whole form clears itself using setRating(0).

05. How to use this page

Help build a better company? Here’s how to use the Feedback module:

STEP 1: QUICK TIPS

If you see a bug in the office or a great new perk idea, just use the **Quick Feedback** form. You don't need a name, just a message.

STEP 2: WATCH THE DEADLINE

Check the **Surveys** section regularly. Most surveys have a deadline (e.g., June 15) and will turn "Completed" after that.

STEP 3: TAKE THE SURVEY

If a survey is **Active**, click the "Take Survey" button. It will open the full question list so your vote can be counted.
